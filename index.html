Web Development

If you decided to have a website for your online business then first step is to understand what kind of website architecture will fit your business and customer needs.

Static Website

Static Web pages contain fixed code; the content of each page does not change unless it is manually updated by the webmaster.

Dynamic Website

Dynamic website can give the website owner the ability to simply update and add new content to the site. For example, news and events could be posted to the site through a simple browser interface.
